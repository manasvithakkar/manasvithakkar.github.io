Introduction

Let me tell you about my journey. I have been in this field since past 7 years. I come from Pune, a city in Indian State Maharashtra. At the age of 16, I decided to become a Computer Engineer. I was inspired by my cousin who was doing her Bachelor's in Computer Engineering. I took a class on C Programming when I was 16. I was the best student in the class. My grip on coding, teacher's compliments and the act of obtaining desired outputs further motivated me. It is since that time I feel that this is where I belong. I have an Associate's degree in Computer Engineering, a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology and by May 2018 I'll have my Master's degree in Computer Science. This has been a wonderful journey until, ofcourse with lots of challenges. I feel that at each stage I have evolved as a programmer. Especially while pursuing my Master's I have learned alot as a programmer and as a person too.

I am an aquaphile. I enjoy all sorts of water sports. Undoubtedly swimming is my favourite hobby! Apart from that I enjoy dancing and cooking. I am a good cook too. I am a foodie and I believe that's a major reason behind me being a good cook! I love travelling and my favorite place is San Francisco.
